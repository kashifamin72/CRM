# Dashboard

The dashboard provides a real-time overview of your sales pipeline.

## KPI Cards (Top Row)
- **Total Leads** — All leads in the system
- **New Leads** — Leads with status "New"
- **Closed Won** — Leads successfully closed
- **Estimated Value** — Sum of all lead estimated values

## Follow-ups Section
- **Today's Pending**: Yellow card showing follow-ups due today
- **Tomorrow's Pending**: Blue card showing follow-ups due tomorrow
- Click any follow-up to open the lead

## Charts
- **Pipeline Bar Chart**: Lead count by status (New → Closed Lost)
- **Source Doughnut Chart**: Lead distribution by source

## Recent Leads
- Last 10 leads sorted by update date
- Columns: Title, Customer, Status (colored badge), Source (colored pill), Value, Assigned To
- Click any lead row to edit

## Manager/Admin Only
- **Sales Officer Filter**: Dropdown at top to filter dashboard by specific officer
- **Lead Distribution**: Progress bars per status
- **Conversion**: Win rate %, won/lost counts
- **Quick Actions**: New Lead, View Leads, Reports
