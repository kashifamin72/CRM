# Follow-ups

## Adding a Follow-up
1. Open a lead's Edit page
2. Go to the **Follow-ups** tab
3. Fill in: Title, Description (optional), Date & Time
4. Click **Schedule Follow-up**

## Follow-up Timeline
The left panel shows all scheduled follow-ups with color-coded borders:
- **Blue border**: Future follow-up
- **Amber border**: Due today
- **Red border**: Overdue
- **Green border**: Completed

### Actions
- ✅ **Mark Complete** — Click the check icon to mark as done
- 🗑️ **Delete** — Remove the follow-up

## Calendar View
Access: **Calendar** in sidebar.

Shows all follow-ups in a month-grid layout.

### Navigation
- **Previous / Next** buttons to change month
- Current month/year displayed in center
- Today's date highlighted with blue circle

### Viewing Follow-ups
- Each day shows up to 4 mini cards
- **Amber cards**: Pending follow-ups
- **Green cards**: Completed follow-ups
- **+N more**: Indicator if more than 4 tasks
- Click any card to open the lead

### Filters (Manager/Admin)
- Sales Officer dropdown to filter by specific officer
- Sales Officers see only their own follow-ups

### Legend
- 🟡 Pending (amber)
- 🟢 Completed (green)
